Create Account

There's no place like Home

What is it?

Sign up for your Chibi 2.0 Account

  • Like in most games - you need an Account
  • It provides you with your unique game ID
  • You can set a mighty name
  • Support via Patreon from here

Set it up !

All it takes is an email address

  • Go to chibifighters.com
  • Click Login / Signup button
  • Enter your email and click create Account
  • Click the link in your email and that's it

Buy Packs

For a Handful of USDT

What is it?

Packs - where your first Chibi 2.0 cards come from

  • To start playing Chibis 2.0 you need to get a pack
  • Right now only Genesis Packs (GP) exist
  • Each of those packs contains 15 cards
  • You will find cards of different types
  • and different rarities in the packs

How to Buy

No more Shady Merchant

  • Login to chibifighters.com and click Buy Packs
  • First, buy some CHIBS (not Chibis)
  • Click on one of the payment methods
  • (coinPayments, ETH, TRX or Paypal)
  • Type the ammount of CHIBS you want and buy
  • Refresh the page and buy your packs

Good to know

Pack a pack or two

  • Allow Popups for the page, else it doesn't work
  • One pack costs 1000 Chibs ~ 5$
  • Cards from packs are not dropped in the game
  • Each edition contains exclusive cards
  • Future packs will only contain 5 cards each
  • The pack supply is limited, Genesis to 23600
  • Rarely packs don't contain Chibis
  • You can buy packs from other players as well!

Open Packs

Just Rip it open !!

What is it?

Packs are a sealed surprise until you open them

  • You can see your GPs in different spots:
    • Account page on chibifighters.com
    • In Game in the top right corner
    • In Game on the Open Packs page
  • Once opened your pack is gone for cards

How to open

Log into the Game and click Open Packs

  • Open a pack by clicking the Open Pack button
  • You will see the back of your cards
  • Reveal by clicking each card
  • Scroll to the right to find all cards
  • Click the red X button to stop this process

Good to know

Surprise in a Surprise !!

  • Browser froze or PC crashed? Don't worry!
  • Once clicked, all cards will be in your collection
  • Want to open many packs? Use the red X button!
  • Rarest cards are found on the left
  • Rarity is decreasing from there to common

Collection

Fill the Heart of Chibis 2.0

What is it?

And here we are - Collectors Heaven !!

  • The Collection displays all your cards
  • Each card displays at the top:
  • Name, Juice and Level
  • Followed by Health, Damage and Image
  • At bottom left the card's Type Number
  • At bottom right the unique Token ID

Your Cards

Wow, there's one, two, three... of me !!

  • Found more than one? Just click it
  • Now you can select the individual card
  • On the left you find options to..
  • ..mint the card to ETH or send it away..
  • ..and upgrade, level and burn the card.
  • Chibis have their equipable slots on the right

Good to know

Might come in handy

  • Burning Genesis cards returns Dust
  • You need Dust to upgrade your cards every 10 level
  • Higher rarities return more dust (1,2,5,10)
  • You can as well use doublettes cards to do so
  • The amount of exp needed rises by 5%/lvl
  • The amount of dust needed rises by 1/20lvl
  • You can get 1 Dust per 24h as a reward
